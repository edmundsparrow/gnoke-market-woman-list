🛒 Market Woman List

A smart offline shopping list for the market. Create trips named by meal or occasion, add items with expected price and quantity, track spending as you shop, and see your total at a glance.

Part of the Gnoke Suite — offline-first tools for everyday Nigerian life. Your data never leaves the device.
